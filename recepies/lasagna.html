How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Heat the pan and add 1 tbsp oil. Saute grated garlic and then add 1 onion diced. Stir till the onion changes the color. Add 3 diced tomatoes and spices and seasoning
  2. Heat pan and add oil. Add spinach (1cup) and stir. Add cheese
  3. Lay lasagna sheets in baking pan. Add tomatoe sauce made in setp 1 on lasagna sheets and add chesse on top. Add another lasagna sheet on top on 1st layer and add spinach sauce. Continue adding another 2 layers and on the last one add cheese on top
