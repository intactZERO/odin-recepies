White sauce pasta

How to make white sauce pasta

Easy to make pasta sauce. This recepie will be a bit on creamy and spicy side

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp White flour
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 cup pasta
  • peri peri masala
  • cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Add 1 tbsp of butter in hot pan. Addd 1 tbsp of while flour when butter is melted. Saute flour for 2 minutes or until flour changes colour.
  2. Add 1 cup of milk and stir until the sauce thickens
  3. Add peri peri masala and cheese
  4. Add boiled pasta and stir into the sauce well
